# Knife Skills

Foundational techniques that make everything else faster and safer.

## Grip

Pinch the blade where it meets the handle with thumb and index finger. Wrap remaining fingers around the handle. The pinch grip gives control — a handle-only grip lets the blade wobble.

## Core cuts

- **dice** — uniform cubes, size specified (brunoise is 3mm, small dice is 6mm)
- **julienne** — thin matchstick strips, about 3mm × 3mm × 5cm
- **chiffonade** — stack leaves, roll tight, slice thin crosswise. Used for herbs and greens.

## The claw

Curl fingertips under on the guiding hand. Knuckles lead, fingertips stay behind the blade. The knife rides against the knuckles — they set the slice thickness.
