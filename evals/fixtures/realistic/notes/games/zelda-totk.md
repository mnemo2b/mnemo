# Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom

Playthrough notes, no full walkthrough.

## Early priorities

- **Unlock all 4 towers** in a region before doing main quests — fast travel saves hours
- **Gather zonai devices** from shrines and drops — fans, wheels, batteries enable vehicle builds
- **Upgrade batteries** at Crystal Refineries — you can't build big until you have batteries to run them
- **Armor upgrades** — find and upgrade a few armor sets you like. Defense compounds.

## Builder mindset

The game rewards creativity. Don't copy other people's designs — think about:
- What do I need? (Move, attack, fly, defend)
- What parts do I have?
- How can I combine them?

Some favorites:
- **Hoverbike** — 2 fans + steering stick, trivializes travel until batteries die
- **Flying bomb** — rocket + time bomb on a shield for range one-shots
- **Mech suit** — for the handful of times a boss needs it

## Shrine tips

- **Read the room** — shrines teach one mechanic each. If you're stuck, you're probably overcomplicating.
- **Fuse is the answer** more often than you'd think
- **Ascend + Recall** solve many puzzles people build ladders for

## Main quest pacing

- Do the 4 regional quests before the final battle
- Don't rush to the castle — endgame content is locked behind progression
- Side quests unlock useful permanent upgrades (battery, armor, recipes)

## What I'd tell a new player

- Experiment aggressively; the game respects weird solutions
- Save before trying stupid builds
- The map is huge; explore instead of grinding
- Don't skip the caves
