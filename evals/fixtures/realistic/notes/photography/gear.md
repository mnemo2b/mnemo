# Photography Gear

Current kit. Added and removed a lot over the years — here's what stays.

## Camera

- **Fujifilm X-T5** — APS-C, 40MP, film simulations
- Light enough to bring daily, quality plenty for everything I shoot

Switched from full-frame; don't miss it. The best camera is the one you actually carry.

## Lenses

- **23mm f/2** — 35mm equivalent, street and everyday
- **56mm f/1.2** — portrait, low light
- **70-300mm** — travel, telephoto when I need it

Three lenses, all different focal lengths, no overlap. If I add a fourth it'll be a wide (16mm).

## Support

- **Peak Design travel tripod** — small, expensive, worth it
- **Leofoto tabletop** — for travel and low-profile work

## Accessories

- Two batteries (never enough with mirrorless)
- 2x fast SD cards (V90 for video)
- Lens cloth, blower, sensor swabs
- L-bracket on the body for fast tripod swapping
- Small prime LED for product and macro

## Bag

- **Peak Design Everyday Sling 10L** — daily
- **Wandrd PRVKE 31L** — travel + laptop

## What I stopped using

- Filters (polarizers, NDs) — hardly used them after buying
- Flash (bounced speedlight for events only)
- Extra camera bodies — one body, two batteries, done
- Strap systems that aren't Peak Design

## Budget priorities

- Lens > body
- Stability (tripod) > more megapixels
- A single great prime > multiple zooms
