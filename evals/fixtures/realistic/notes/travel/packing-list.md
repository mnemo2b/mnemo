# Packing List

My default packing list. Adjusts for climate and length of trip.

## Carry-on (always)

- Passport (+ physical photocopy in checked bag)
- Wallet, cards, cash in multiple currencies
- Phone + charger + cable
- Laptop + charger (if needed)
- Noise-canceling headphones
- Eye mask, earplugs
- Snacks + refillable water bottle
- Change of clothes (in case of lost luggage)
- Basic meds: painkillers, antihistamine, imodium, melatonin

## Clothes (per week)

- 3-4 shirts (mix t-shirts and button-downs)
- 2 pairs pants / shorts
- 1 light jacket or overshirt
- 1 nice outfit for dinner out
- 7 underwear, 7 socks
- Packable rain layer
- Sleepwear

I pack in 3-day modules: wash on day 4, re-wear into day 6.

## Toiletries

- Travel bottles (refilled, TSA-compliant)
- Toothbrush, toothpaste (travel size)
- Deodorant
- Razor
- Sunscreen (TSA-OK if <100ml)
- Contacts + solution, backup glasses

## Tech

- Universal adapter
- Power bank (> 10,000 mAh)
- Cable organizer (you'll thank yourself)
- eSIM or unlock phone for local SIM

## Trip-specific

- **Beach** — swim trunks, reef-safe sunscreen, flip-flops, dry bag
- **Hiking** — trail shoes, moisture-wicking layers, first aid, blister patches
- **Cold** — thermal base, wool socks, gloves, beanie, packable down
- **Business** — blazer, dress shoes, laptop cables, mini steamer

## What I stopped packing

- Books (Kindle earns its weight)
- Multiple pairs of shoes (one daily + one alternate max)
- Formal outfits "just in case"
- Paper maps (offline Google Maps + Maps.me)
