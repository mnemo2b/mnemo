# Cleaning Routines

What keeps the house tolerable without weekend deep-cleans.

## Daily (5-10 min total)

- Dishes into dishwasher / hand-wash immediately after cooking
- Kitchen counters wiped down
- One load of laundry started if needed
- 2-minute tidy before bed (surfaces, cushions)

## Weekly (30-45 min)

- Vacuum floors (every area)
- Bathrooms — toilet, sink, mirror, shower
- Mop kitchen floor
- Change sheets
- Trash out

## Monthly

- Oven
- Inside of fridge
- Baseboards
- Dust ceiling fans and light fixtures
- Deep clean shower (descaler)

## Quarterly

- Windows
- Clean under furniture
- Wash curtains
- Pantry and spice cabinet audit

## Principles

- **Don't put it down, put it away** — reduces the "stuff collects" problem
- **One in, one out** — especially for clothes, kitchen gadgets, books
- **Tools where you use them** — kitchen cleaner under the kitchen sink, bathroom cleaner in each bathroom
- **Set a timer** — 15 min of dedicated cleaning beats 2 hr of half-attention

## What I don't do

- Shampoo carpets (vacuum + spot-clean is enough for most)
- Elaborate product rotations (3 cleaners max — kitchen, bathroom, glass)
- Cleaning schedules I don't follow (rules I break are worse than no rules)
