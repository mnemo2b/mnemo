# Home Maintenance

Checklists beat memory.

## Monthly

- HVAC filters (quarterly for lightly-used; monthly if pets)
- Run garbage disposal with ice + citrus
- Check smoke/CO detectors (test button)

## Quarterly

- Flush water heater sediment (tankless: descale annually)
- Garbage disposal deep clean
- Replace range hood filter
- Test GFCI outlets

## Annually

- HVAC service (spring for AC, fall for furnace)
- Gutters cleaned
- Dryer vent cleaned — actual fire risk
- Water heater anode rod inspection
- Re-caulk bathtubs and sinks as needed
- Check and replace weatherstripping
- Exterior wood inspection (decks, fences)

## As needed

- Grout sealant every 1-2 years
- Garage door lubrication
- Deck staining every 2-3 years
- Pressure washing driveways, siding

## Tools worth owning

- Cordless drill + basic bit set
- Stud finder
- Level (2' and a small torpedo)
- Shop vac
- Ladder (6' minimum)
- Plunger + snake
- Caulk gun
- Putty knife

## When to call a pro

- Anything involving gas lines
- Electrical past a light switch swap
- Roofing, foundation, structural
- HVAC past filter changes
- Plumbing behind walls
