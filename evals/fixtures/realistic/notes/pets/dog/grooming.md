# Dog Grooming

Home grooming routine.

## Schedule

- **Daily** — teeth (or every other day at minimum)
- **Weekly** — brush, ears checked, nails checked
- **Monthly** — bath, nails trimmed, deep ear clean
- **Quarterly** — full groom (or professional)

## Brushing

- Slicker brush for medium/long coats
- Undercoat rake for double-coats, during shed seasons
- Pin brush for light daily passes
- Mat comb for tangles (before they mat)

Brush before bathing — matted fur becomes worse when wet.

## Nails

- Clip when you hear them click on hard floors
- Small cuts weekly beat big cuts monthly (quick recedes over time)
- Styptic powder on hand for accidents
- Dremel / grinder if dog won't tolerate clippers — takes desensitization

## Bathing

- Dog shampoo only (human shampoo is too alkaline)
- Cool-ish water, not hot
- Rinse thoroughly — residue itches
- Towel dry, blow dry on low/cool for heavy coats

## Ears

- Weekly check for redness, odor, discharge
- Pet-safe ear cleaner if floppy ears (moisture traps)
- Never insert cotton swabs deep
- Odor + head-shaking + scratching = vet

## Teeth

- Dog toothpaste (never human — xylitol is toxic)
- Start slow, positive reinforcement
- Dental chews are a supplement, not replacement
- Annual cleaning under anesthesia at vet for most dogs past 3-4 years old
