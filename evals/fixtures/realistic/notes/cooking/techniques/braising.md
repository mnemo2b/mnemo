# Braising

Tough cuts → tender dinner. Low heat, partial liquid, long time. Collagen breaks down into gelatin, which is what makes braises unctuous.

## Formula

1. **Sear hard** — fond is flavor, don't crowd the pan
2. **Sweat aromatics** — onion + carrot + celery, maybe garlic
3. **Deglaze** — wine, stock, or water, scrape the fond
4. **Braising liquid** — reaches 1/3 up the meat, not submerged
5. **Cover and cook low** — 135-160°C oven, or barely bubbling on stovetop
6. **Temperature target** — 85-90°C internal for collagen breakdown
7. **Rest** — 20-30 min, or cool overnight (better next day)

## Time

- Short ribs: 3-4 hr
- Lamb shanks: 2-3 hr
- Pork shoulder: 3-4 hr
- Oxtail: 3-4 hr

## Notes

Strain the sauce and reduce hard if it's watery. A knob of cold butter at the end emulsifies and shines.
