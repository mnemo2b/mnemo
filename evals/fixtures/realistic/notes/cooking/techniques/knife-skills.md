# Knife Skills

The four cuts that cover 90% of home cooking: rough chop, dice, julienne, brunoise.

## Grip

- Pinch blade with thumb and index finger
- Remaining fingers wrap handle
- Guiding hand: fingers curled, knuckles forward, blade rides against knuckles

## Cuts

- **Rough chop** — uneven, ok for stocks and long braises
- **Dice** — large (2cm), medium (1cm), small (5mm)
- **Julienne** — matchsticks, ~3mm × 3mm × 5cm
- **Brunoise** — dice from julienne, ~3mm cubes

## Technique

Rock cuts for round/wedgy things (onions, carrots). Slide cuts for hard things (butternut). Tap cuts for herbs (stop squishing them).

## Maintenance

- Hone before every session (realigns edge)
- Sharpen every few months (removes metal to create a new edge)
- Wood or plastic board only, never glass or stone
