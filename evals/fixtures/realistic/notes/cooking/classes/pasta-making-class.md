# Pasta-Making Class — Bologna, 2024

Two hours with Anna, 60-year sfoglina. Learned to hand-roll sfoglia for tagliatelle, tortellini, and pappardelle.

## Dough

- **Base ratio** — 100g "00" flour + 1 egg per serving. Yolks only for richer pasta.
- No water, no salt, no oil. Just flour and eggs.
- Knead 10 min until glossy
- Rest 30 min, covered, at room temp

## Rolling (sfoglia)

- Long wooden pin (mattarello), no rolling pin with handles
- Roll from center outward, rotating dough
- Target: thin enough to read newsprint through
- Dust with semolina (not flour) to prevent sticking

## Shapes

### Tagliatelle

- Roll sfoglia into loose cylinder
- Cut 8mm wide with a sharp knife
- Unroll immediately, nest in loose bundles

### Tortellini

- Cut sfoglia into 4cm squares
- Pea-size filling (prosciutto, mortadella, parmigiano, nutmeg)
- Fold diagonally, press edges
- Wrap around finger, press corners together

### Pappardelle

- Cut 2-3cm wide
- Irregular edges with a rolling cutter look best

## Serve

- Cook 2-3 min in heavily salted water
- Pasta water is sauce-making liquid, reserve 1 cup
- Finish pasta in the sauce, never serve sauce on top of drained pasta
