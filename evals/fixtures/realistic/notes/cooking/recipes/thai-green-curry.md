# Thai Green Curry

Green curry (gaeng keow wan) is made with fresh green chilies, coriander roots, and lots of fresh aromatics. Herbal and bright, unlike the earthier red curry.

## Paste

- green bird's eye chilies (10-15)
- coriander roots and stems (key flavor)
- galangal, lemongrass, makrut lime zest
- shallots, garlic
- white pepper, shrimp paste
- cumin, coriander seed (toasted)

Pound in mortar in order of hardness. Takes 20-30 minutes to get right.

## Curry

1. Fry paste in coconut cream (the thick part) until oil separates — ~5 min
2. Add protein (chicken thigh, fish balls, or tofu), coat in paste
3. Add thinner coconut milk and stock
4. Simmer 15-20 min
5. Add vegetables (Thai eggplant, bamboo shoots) in the last 5 min
6. Season with fish sauce and palm sugar
7. Finish with Thai basil and torn makrut lime leaves

## Notes

Coconut cream must split (oil separates out) or the flavor stays flat. Don't skip fresh coriander root.
