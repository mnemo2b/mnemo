# Mushroom Risotto

Creamy Italian rice dish. The mantecatura (finishing with butter and cheese off-heat) is what separates restaurant from home versions.

## Ingredients

- 320g carnaroli or arborio rice
- 300g mixed mushrooms (porcini, chestnut, shiitake)
- 30g dried porcini
- 1L hot mushroom/chicken stock (kept on a simmer)
- 1 shallot, finely diced
- 150ml dry white wine
- 50g cold butter, cubed
- 50g parmigiano reggiano, grated
- thyme

## Steps

1. Rehydrate dried porcini in warm water; strain liquid into stock
2. Sauté fresh mushrooms hard and set aside
3. Sweat shallot in butter until translucent
4. Toast rice in the pan, 1-2 min
5. Deglaze with wine, let absorb
6. Ladle hot stock, stirring often, until each ladle is absorbed — ~18 min total
7. Rice should be al dente, with a slight wave (all'onda)
8. Off heat, beat in cold butter and parmigiano — mantecatura
9. Fold in mushrooms, rest 2 min before serving

## Notes

Don't wash the rice. Don't add cream. Stock must be hot or you halt the cook.
