# Pasta Carbonara

Roman pasta dish. The sauce is egg and cheese, not cream.

## Ingredients

- 400g guanciale
- 6 egg yolks
- 200g pecorino romano
- 400g rigatoni
- black pepper

## Steps

1. Cut guanciale into strips, render in a cold pan over medium heat
2. Cook pasta in salted water until 1 minute short of al dente
3. Whisk yolks with grated pecorino and pepper
4. Toss hot pasta with guanciale and rendered fat, off heat
5. Add egg mixture, toss quickly — residual heat cooks the eggs without scrambling

## Notes

Guanciale > pancetta > bacon. Pecorino, not parmesan. The trick is temperature control: pan must be hot enough to thicken but not hot enough to scramble.
