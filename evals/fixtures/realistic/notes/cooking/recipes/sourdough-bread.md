# Sourdough Bread

Basic country loaf. Hydration ~75%, overnight bulk, cold retard.

## Schedule

- Morning: feed starter
- Afternoon: mix dough when starter is peaked (~4-6 hr after feed)
- Evening: bulk ferment + folds (4-5 hr at room temp)
- Night: shape, cold retard in fridge overnight
- Next morning: bake from cold

## Ingredients

- 500g bread flour (or 450g bread + 50g whole wheat)
- 375g water (75% hydration)
- 100g active starter
- 10g salt

## Process

1. Autolyse flour + water, 45 min
2. Add starter and salt, pinch and fold to incorporate
3. Bulk ferment with 4 sets of stretch-and-folds every 30 min
4. Continue bulk until dough has risen 50-70%, jiggly and domed
5. Pre-shape on unfloured counter, rest 20 min
6. Final shape, place in banneton, fridge overnight
7. Bake from cold in a preheated dutch oven at 250°C, 20 min lid on, 20 min lid off

## Notes

Starter strength matters more than timing. Cold retard develops flavor and makes scoring cleaner.
