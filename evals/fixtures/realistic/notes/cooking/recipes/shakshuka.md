# Shakshuka

North African eggs poached in spiced tomato sauce. Weekend breakfast staple.

## Ingredients

- 1 onion, diced
- 1 red bell pepper, diced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tsp each: cumin, sweet paprika, smoked paprika
- pinch chili flakes
- 800g canned whole tomatoes, hand-crushed
- 4-6 eggs
- 100g feta
- fresh parsley, cilantro

## Steps

1. Sauté onion and pepper in olive oil until soft, ~10 min
2. Add garlic and spices, toast 30 sec
3. Add tomatoes with juice, simmer 15 min until thick
4. Season aggressively with salt
5. Make wells, crack eggs into them
6. Cover and cook on low 5-7 min until whites set but yolks runny
7. Crumble feta, scatter herbs, serve with crusty bread

## Notes

Don't stir once eggs are in. Better tomatoes = better shakshuka. Harissa elevates it.
