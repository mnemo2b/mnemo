# Roast Chicken

Whole roast chicken, high-heat method. Crispy skin, juicy meat, no brine needed if you dry-salt overnight.

## Prep

- 1.5-2kg whole chicken
- salt (~1 tsp per kg)
- butter or olive oil
- aromatics: lemon, thyme, garlic

Dry-salt all surfaces 24 hr ahead, rest uncovered on a rack in the fridge. This dries the skin (crispier) and seasons the meat.

## Roast

1. Take chicken out 1 hr before to temper
2. Oven at 230°C (450°F)
3. Stuff cavity with halved lemon, thyme, smashed garlic
4. Truss legs if you want even cooking, or spatchcock for faster
5. Rub skin with butter, place breast-up on a rack over potatoes/veg
6. Roast ~45-55 min until thigh hits 74°C
7. Rest 15 min before carving

## Notes

Rest or you lose juices. Put potatoes under the bird — they absorb the fat and become the best part of the meal.
