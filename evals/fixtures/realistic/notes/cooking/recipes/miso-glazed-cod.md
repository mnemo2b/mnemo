# Miso-Glazed Cod

Black cod with saikyo miso glaze. Marinate at least overnight — 2 days is better.

## Marinade

- 100g white miso (saikyo, or mix regular white with 1 tbsp sugar)
- 3 tbsp mirin
- 3 tbsp sake
- 2 tbsp sugar

Simmer sake and mirin briefly to cook off alcohol, stir into miso and sugar.

## Method

1. Pat cod fillets dry, coat generously with marinade
2. Wrap in parchment or cheesecloth, refrigerate 24-48 hr
3. Scrape off most of the marinade (leave a thin coating)
4. Broil 6-8 min until glaze blisters and fish flakes
5. Rest 1 min, serve with pickled ginger

## Notes

Cheaper cod works but black cod (sablefish) is the original. Don't skip the cheesecloth — it prevents burning on the broiler.
