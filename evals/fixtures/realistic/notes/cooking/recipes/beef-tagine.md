# Beef Tagine with Prunes and Almonds

Moroccan slow-cooked stew, sweet and savory. The prunes and honey balance against warming spices.

## Ingredients

- 1kg beef chuck, cut into large chunks
- 2 onions, thinly sliced
- 4 cloves garlic
- 1 tsp each: ginger, cumin, cinnamon, sweet paprika
- pinch saffron in warm water
- 200g prunes (soaked in warm water)
- 100g whole blanched almonds (toasted)
- 2 tbsp honey
- 1 tbsp orange blossom water (optional)
- sesame seeds to garnish

## Steps

1. Brown beef in batches in a tagine or heavy pot
2. Soften onions, add garlic and spices
3. Return beef, add water to just cover, saffron
4. Cover and simmer 2 hours on lowest heat (or oven at 150°C)
5. Add prunes and honey in the last 30 min
6. Finish with almonds and sesame seeds

## Notes

If using a ceramic tagine, use a heat diffuser on gas. Real tagines don't need much liquid — the cone captures steam and drips it back.
