# Writing Portfolio — Content Plan

## Cadence

- **Essays** — monthly, target 1500-3000 words, high effort
- **Notes** — weekly, 500-1500 words, looser
- **Now page** — quarterly
- **Reading page** — ongoing as I read

## Essay types (+ pipeline)

- Technical deep-dives (longest, best for portfolio credibility)
- Personal essays (hardest to get right; emotional risk)
- Opinion / takes (quickest to write, shortest shelf life)
- Process notes ("how I do X")

Aim for 2:1:1:1 mix across a year.

## Distribution

- RSS (always)
- Newsletter (every publish)
- Mastodon + Bluesky (selectively, with brief commentary)
- No Twitter/X
- Hacker News (when I think a piece fits — rarely)

## Measuring

- Pageviews — directionally useful, not the goal
- Time on page — does suggest real reading
- Newsletter replies — strongest signal
- Inbound links + cites — the real bar

## What I avoid

- Clickbait headlines
- Publishing to hit a calendar slot
- Engagement-farming opinions
- Dashboard obsession — the work is writing, not metrics
