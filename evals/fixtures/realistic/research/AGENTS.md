# Research

Raw notes and source summaries from ongoing research. Less polished than `notes/` — this is where I dump findings before distilling them.

## Organization

Topics mirror `notes/` where they overlap, so I can compare what I've researched against what I've actually internalized. Expect unfinished thoughts, direct quotes, and links to source material.

## Conventions

- files often start with the research question as the heading
- keep sources linked at the bottom
- promote to `notes/` once a topic is well-understood
